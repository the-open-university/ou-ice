Academic Search Complete

Database: A multi-disciplinary full-text database, with nearly 6,000 full-text periodicals, including more than 5,000 peer-reviewed journals. In addition to full text, this database offers abstracts for over 10,000 journals books, reports and conference proceedings.

IEEE Xplore

Database: The full text of all papers, many going back to the 1860s, including the journals, transactions, magazines and conference proceedings of the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers (IEEE) and the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) - formerly the Institution of Electrical Engineers (IEE) - plus approximately 900 standards and the standards dictionary. We recommend using the Advanced Search for full-text searching

Science Direct

Database: The full texts of over 1000 scientific, technical, medical, mathematical, management and social science journals. 1995 onwards. Provides e-mail 'alerts' to update searches and announce the arrival of latest journal issues. 'Cited by' facility lists papers that have cited any article displayed on the screen. Includes access to selected full-text encyclopedias. (You will be asked to register on your first visit only).
