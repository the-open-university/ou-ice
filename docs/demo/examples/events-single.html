London Alumni Networking Informal Drop In

Sep 13

Tuesday, September 13, 2016

18:30 to 21:30

The King’s Head, 10 Stafford Street, London, W1S 4RX

Contact: Kapil Dev

This is an informal event in a pub in Central London. Pop in for a quick drink or stay for longer. Friends and colleagues are welcome.

This event takes place in The King's Head every second Tuesday each month

Nearest tube & rail stations:

  • Green Park (350 metres - Jubilee, Piccadilly and Victoria lines)
  • Piccadilly (500 metres - Bakerloo and Piccadilly lines)
  • Oxford Circus (900 metres - Central, Victoria and Bakerloo lines)
  • Charing Cross (1.1 km - Northern, Bakerloo and National Rail lines)

Future Dates:

  • Tuesday 11 October 2016
