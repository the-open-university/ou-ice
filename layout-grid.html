With iPad responsiveness

If your layout has 3 or 4 columns, each column of equal width, you can add the class "ou-cols-3" or "ou-cols-4" to the container which gives you iPad responsiveness (between 767px and 400px your 3 or 4 columns become 2 columns). Resize the browser window to see the difference.
