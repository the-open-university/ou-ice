Student Cafe Forum

Purpose of this forum

Please take a few moments to familiarise yourself with these guidelines. This Student Cafe forum is for you to informally chat with the other students studying the module with you. While it is moderated it is not the place to post queries about module content or activities. Your tutor is the first point of contact for this type of query.

You can read any posts within this forum, but not submit your own posts.
