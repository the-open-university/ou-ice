Databases

Access a wide range of information sources, including full-text journals, ebooks, images and video, abstracts and indexes, statistics and financial data by using the relevant link in the list below.

Some databases might require specific usernames and passwords. Some do not give access to the full-text of articles.

Taylor & Francis e-book collection

Database: The full text of selected titles subscribed to by the OU, across a range of subject areas including arts, business, social sciences, health and science.

Taylor & Francis Journals Complete

Database: Full text articles from journals subscribed to by the OU. Search by keyword or browse by subject.

IEEE Xplore

Database: The full text of all papers, many going back to the 1860s, including the journals, transactions, magazines and conference proceedings of the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineers (IEEE) and the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) - formerly the Institution of Electrical Engineers (IEE) - plus approximately 900 standards and the standards dictionary. We recommend using the Advanced Search for full-text searching

Teachers TV - Education in Video

Database: Teachers TV was a government funded programme producing instructional videos showing actual classroom footage from 2008 to 2011. The 3,500 programmes are freely available, though all content is protected by Crown Copyright.

TELECOMMUNICATIONSnetBASE

Database: The full texts, cross-searchable, of handbooks in all areas of telecommunication

Television and Radio Index for Learning and Teaching (TRILT)

Database: An extensive online database of UK television and radio programmes including, terrestrial, cable, and satellite television, all national and many local radio stations. Data is available at least 10 days before transmission.
