Law cases

Case law reports only contain details of court judgments for cases which raise a point of legal significance.

UK Cases

  • If you don't know which law report a case appears in you can use JustCite to locate it.  If you are searching for cases on a topic, try LexisLibrary or Westlaw first, as they cover a large number of different law reports. If you are looking for a pre 1865 case try HeinOnline, Justis, LexisLibrary or Westlaw.
  • Each case is given a 'citation', which includes an abbreviation of the law report containing the case. Cardiff Index to Legal Abbreviations will help you find the full title of the law report.
  • For unreported cases try Lawtel.
  • For recent decisions, The Times newspaper on Nexis UK reports decisions a day or so after the case is heard.
  • The location of the main individual law reports is listed below.

UK Law

BAILII : British and Irish Legal Information institute

Database: Freely available access to British and Irish case law & legislation, European Union case law, Law Commission reports, and other law-related British and Irish material.

Family Law Online

Database: Full text access to three journals, reports, and legal publications in family law, published by Jordans.

HeinOnline

Database: Mainly covers US law, but increasingly UK journals are being included. Gives access to a Law Journal Library, the Federal Register Library, Treaties and Agreements Library and US Supreme Court Library.

ICLR Online

Database: Searchable database of UK law reports and case law provided by the Incorporated Council for Law Reporting for England & Wales

JustCite

Database: JustCite is a legal search engine and citator service that cross-references case law, legislation and journal articles

Lawtel

Database: Case law, PI Quantum Reports, statutory law, Statutory Instruments, Parliamentary Bills, Command Papers, practice directions and an index of articles from law journals. With many internal links to related material. Now includes Lawtel Human Rights. Updated daily.

EU and worldwide Law

EUR-Lex : the portal to European Union law

Database: Designed to keep users up to date on legislation in force in the European Union and new legislation as it is enacted, with the intention to make EU law more accessible to the general public. The site has 8 sections: Treaties, the Official Journal, Legislation, Legislation in preparation, Parliamentary questions, Documents of public interest, Preparing Enlargement and Case-law. The site is searchable and is available in all official European Community languages. The full text of many documents is available to download

International Law Reports via Justis

Database: Full text of International law reports from 1902 to the present day. To search, type international in the search box and select International Law Reports from the drop down options. You can now search this collection by entering your search terms. Alternatively to browse click on Index and Contents, then Cases, then All Cases

Lexis Library

Database: Provides access to UK and EU legislation, cases (including the All England Reports) and journals. Also contains Harvey on Industrial Relations and Employment Law and Halsbury's Laws of England

Westlaw UK (via Athens)

Database: Legislation, cases and law journals for the UK, EU and the US. Access to material relating to the EU can be accessed via the 'EU' option and US material from 'Services' then 'Westlaw International'. With an index of legal terms that gives definitions, links to cases, suggested terms and related searches.

WorldLII : World Legal Information institute

Database: Freely available access to global case law & legislation, for 123 jurisdictions
