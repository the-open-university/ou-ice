Welcome to The OU Law School

The law degree offered by The Open University Law School is a major innovation in legal education. It is a Qualifying Law Degree (QLD) approved by the legal professional bodies.

The Open University is a world leader in providing flexible supported open learning. Our law course materials have been prepared with great expertise to allow students to progress smoothly through difficult areas of law. Our law students can access an impressive range of law reports, articles and other legal materials online through The Open University library.

A wide range of people study law with us. The wonderful richness of life in our tutor groups gives very helpful added dimension to the study of legal issues. Read more
