Every OU web page needfs to carry one of the standard headers and footers. There are three to choose from within this framework:

  • Student header and footer - for use on student facing websites
  • Tutor header and footer - for use on TutorHome and associated pages
  • Public header and footer - for use on pages that do not require authentication and/or aimed at the general public
